I'm a Data Science major at UC San Diego (graduating June 2026) with a Minor in Cognitive Science. My work lives at the intersection of machine learning, cloud infrastructure, and interpretability — building systems that don't just perform well, but can be understood and trusted.

I've deployed deep learning models to production on AWS and GCP, automated logistics pipelines with Python, and built Tableau dashboards for real-time operational decision-making. I care as much about how a model behaves as how accurate it is.
